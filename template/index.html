ROBERT PATTINSON

About Robert Pattinson

Robert Pattinson is an English actor, model, and musician, born on May 13, 1986, in London, England. He gained widespread fame and recognition for his role as Edward Cullen in the film adaptations of Stephenie Meyer's "Twilight" series.

Pattinson's breakthrough role came in 2008 when he portrayed Edward Cullen, a brooding and mysterious vampire, in the movie "Twilight." The film was based on the first book of the Twilight Saga and was an enormous commercial success, catapulting Pattinson to international stardom. He reprised his role as Edward Cullen in the subsequent films: "The Twilight Saga: New Moon" (2009), "The Twilight Saga: Eclipse" (2010), "The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn - Part 1" (2011), and "The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn - Part 2" (2012).

Despite the immense fame he gained from "Twilight," Robert Pattinson actively sought out more diverse and challenging roles to establish himself as a versatile actor. He appeared in various independent and mainstream films, receiving critical acclaim for his performances.

Throughout his career, Robert Pattinson has been recognized for his talent and has been nominated for and won several awards. He has also taken on roles in big-budget films, including playing Bruce Wayne/Batman in the DC Extended Universe (DCEU) film "The Batman," which was slated for release in 2022.

Movies Of Robert Pattinson

As of my last update in September 2021, Robert Pattinson has appeared in numerous films, showcasing his versatility as an actor. Here are some of his notable movies up to that point:

  1. Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (2005) - Pattinson played the role of Cedric Diggory, a Hogwarts student, and champion in the Triwizard Tournament.
  2. Twilight (2008) - This film marked his breakthrough role as Edward Cullen, a vampire who falls in love with a human girl named Bella Swan.
  3. The Twilight Saga sequels - After the first film, Robert Pattinson reprised his role as Edward Cullen in "The Twilight Saga: New Moon" (2009), "The Twilight Saga: Eclipse" (2010), "The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn - Part 1" (2011), and "The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn - Part 2" (2012).
  4. Remember Me (2010) - Pattinson starred in this romantic drama as a young man dealing with personal tragedies while falling in love.
  5. Water for Elephants (2011) - He appeared in this period drama alongside Reese Witherspoon, portraying a veterinary student who joins a traveling circus.
  6. Cosmopolis (2012) - Pattinson took on the lead role of a young billionaire navigating through a surreal and tumultuous day in New York City.
  7. The Rover (2014) - This post-apocalyptic drama featured Pattinson as a man helping a loner (played by Guy Pearce) recover his stolen car in a lawless Australian outback.
  8. Maps to the Stars (2014) - He played the role of a limo driver in this dark satire about the lives of Hollywood celebrities.
  9. Life (2015) - Pattinson starred as photographer Dennis Stock in this biographical drama about his friendship with actor James Dean (played by Dane DeHaan).
  10. The Lost City of Z (2016) - In this adventure drama, he portrayed a British corporal accompanying explorer Percy Fawcett (played by Charlie Hunnam) on a journey through the Amazon.
  11. Good Time (2017) - Pattinson received critical acclaim for his performance as a bank robber on the run in this crime thriller.
  12. High Life (2018) - He played a convict on a space voyage in this sci-fi drama directed by Claire Denis.
  13. The Lighthouse (2019) - Pattinson starred alongside Willem Dafoe in this psychological horror film, playing a young lighthouse keeper.
  14. Tenet (2020) - He had a prominent role in Christopher Nolan's sci-fi action film as Neil, a mysterious ally to the protagonist.
